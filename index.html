How would feel? when would play a shooting game as if its real? Won't you love to shoot with your fingers like you used to when you were a child, how about bringing all your imagination to reality. Well Game-X is a browser integrated shooting game, which works on real life movements and physical efforts of the person playing it, rather then just banging on keys of his keyboard or moving his mouse. It integrates body movement with movement recognition and hand movement with shooting recognition by using just your webcam( No other sophisticated gadgets are required). That means, you control the person in the game with your body movements rather then using mouse and keyboard.

Game X is an online browser integrated shooting game which runs by operating defferent servers. Each server runs the game and consists of a group of registered players. Players are either priests or marauders. The weapon range is from medieval o modern. The game is based on gesture recognition technology in which the person or the registered player sits in front of his webcam with his both hands infront of him. Before the game starts, the application recognizes his face, shoulders and hands.
